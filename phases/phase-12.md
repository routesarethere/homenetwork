## Phase 12 — Feb 2026

My SG1016PE switch and 9u rack had both become quite full. TP-Link released the SG2428LP — a 28-port switch with 16 PoE ports, fanless, which is very important to me. The Easy Smart interface on the SG1016PE was always a bit cumbersome, so moving to an Omada-supported switch was a nice upgrade on top of the extra capacity.

I also picked up a GL.iNet Comet PoE KVM to play with. If it turns out to be useful enough, I may get one for each of my physical hosts in the rack.

Speaking of the rack — I found a 21u Tripp Lite on Facebook Marketplace, and the seller turned out to be an old friend. Funny how life works out sometimes.

As of today, the move is complete. Everything is in the 21u and it's a relief to finally have room to breathe again.
