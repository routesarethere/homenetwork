## Phase 3 — Jan 2023

Up to this point, my basement "rack" had been living on a card table. I ordered a 9u rack from Monoprice but again lacked some experience — this time around how to mount the rack and make sure it doesn't fall.

I called up a friend who was thankfully happy to come assist. We could either drill into the concrete block wall or bolt it to the floor joists above. We went with the latter, building a 2×4 backing for the rack to mount to that hangs from the floor joists.

With the rack mounted, cabling run, and wifi in the garage — I started feeling really accomplished with this project.
