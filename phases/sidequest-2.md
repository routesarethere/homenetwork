## Side Quest 2 — Mar 2025

Twelve terabytes: also laughable. I ordered two 24TB drives.

Using the same approach as before — I turned off camera recording, pulled the single 4TB drive in bay 4, and used the open bay to slot in the 24TB drives one at a time, replacing one of the 4TB drives and one of the 12TB drives. Once that finished, I formatted one of the freed 12TB drives, popped it into bay 4, and resumed camera recording.

I also configured the \*arr stack to store any media *not* requested by me to the single-drive pool. That way my personally curated stuff stays on the redundant SHR pool, and the bulk automated downloads live separately.
