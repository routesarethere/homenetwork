## Side Quest 1 — Feb 2024

Four terabytes turned out to be laughable. I ordered two 12TB drives.

I put both new 12TB drives and one of the 4TB drives into one SHR storage pool (bays 1, 2, 3). I then put the remaining 4TB drive into its own basic storage pool (bay 4) dedicated to camera footage — that way I could write 24/7 without worrying about the impact on the drives storing my important files and media.

If I ever need to replace or upgrade a drive (spoiler alert: I will — 12TB will become laughable too), I can turn off camera recording and temporarily pull the single drive in bay 4. That frees up the bay to slot in a new drive and run a "replace" in the NAS without degrading the storage pool. Once the replaced drive is out, I reinstall the camera drive and resume recording.
