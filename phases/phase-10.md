## Phase 10 — Apr 2025

With 3 cats, we wanted some interior cameras to monitor key areas — like the litter boxes — so we could observe any concerning behaviors or fighting. I picked up four Reolink E1 Pro cameras (Wi-Fi). Normally I'd want wired PoE cameras, but these may need to move and be adjusted depending on how rooms are laid out. They're a nicety, not mission-critical for surveillance.

I also purchased a Reolink RLN36 NVR to write footage to. It was cheaper than paying for all the needed Synology Surveillance Station licenses, I can add more cameras in the future without paying more, and I already had a 4TB drive sitting on the shelf.

On the wireless side, I scooped a UAP-AC-Pro from a friend who was selling. It went in the living room, and I moved the AC-Lite up to the 2nd story — better coverage all around.
