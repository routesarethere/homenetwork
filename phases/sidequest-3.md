## Side Quest 3 — Feb 2026

With nearly full drives in both storage pools and the boom of AI driving up drive prices and demand, I decided to snag one more 24TB drive to replace the remaining 12TB in bay 3. All three drives in the SHR pool are now 24TB.

That also freed up the 12TB drive, which went into the NVR — allowing significantly more camera footage to be retained.

Twenty-four terabytes will probably become laughable too. But not today.
