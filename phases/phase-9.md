## Phase 9 — Mar 2025

In order to assist with media acquisition, I set up the \*arr stack: Radarr, Sonarr, Bazarr, Prowlarr, Sabnzbd, and Overseerr (now Seerr).

I can't believe I didn't look into this sooner. Nearly any piece of media I could want was now at my fingertips, and I could easily set my friends up to request things themselves.
